Child Safety & CSAE Standards

At LoviX, operated by Evolve Solution, we are committed to protecting children and preventing any form of Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE).

Zero-Tolerance Policy

We maintain a zero-tolerance policy toward any content, behavior, or activity that involves child sexual abuse or exploitation.

Prohibited Content

The following content is strictly prohibited:

  • Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM)
  • Sexual content involving minors
  • Grooming or sexual exploitation of children
  • Any attempt to create, distribute, or promote CSAE content

Detection & Enforcement

We actively monitor our platform and take immediate action when CSAE-related content or behavior is identified, including:

  • Immediate content removal
  • Permanent account suspension or termination
  • Reporting to relevant law enforcement authorities where required
